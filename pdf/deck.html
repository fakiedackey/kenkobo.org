04 / Service deep‑dive · 02

High‑Fidelity Simulators

Commercial market‑data feeds publish order‑book snapshots every 500 ms. Between snapshots, the order flow is invisible — and naive simulators fill that gap with assumptions that diverge from live behaviour.

snapshot t ┐ │ modelled │ solution │ space snapshot t+1 ┘ → order‑level reconstruction → sim‑to‑prod parity
  • Reconstruct the feasible order‑level state between snapshots by modelling the solution space, not interpolating.
  • Backtest PnL closely tracks live deployment instead of diverging on contact with the market.
  • Resulted in significant uplift in simulator quality and downstream PnL of strategies trained on it.
